My Poem:-

This is the begging of the

Tumi amar robe nirobe thako,

bondhu hoye abong taro khache,

ami chai abhebei thakteh,

naa naam nah thikana,

tobuo moner khache.

Bondhu hoye mone rekho please ,

jodi sujog deo tahole aro khache ,

nahole noy.

tomar sathe thakle jeno mon theke bhalo thaki

atukuei mone hoy.
